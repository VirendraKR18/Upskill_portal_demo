1. Objective

Design and implement a credit-based learning platform to upskill organizational resources in AI, enabling measurable progress, gamified engagement, and career advancement, ultimately transforming the organization into an AI-native enterprise.

2. Business Goals
Build AI competency across engineering teams
Introduce measurable and verifiable learning credits
Improve employee engagement via gamification
Enable certification pathways through approved providers
Align learning outcomes with career progression
Provide leadership visibility into skill development
3. Scope
In Scope
Web-based learning platform
Credit-based system for tracking progress
Gamification (badges, ranks, leaderboards)
Certification workflows
Integration with Org SSO
Reporting & analytics dashboards
Audit-ready credit system
Out of Scope (Phase 1)
Mobile application
Deep LMS integration (future phase)
External marketplace integrations beyond approved providers
